Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Who doesn't like chocolate chip cookies? This recipe will take you through the essential process of making chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda, salt
  3. Cream together butter, white sugar, brown sugar until smooth
  4. Blend in eggs and vanilla
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until blended
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips using a wooden spoon
  7. Scoop cookies and place at least 2 inches apart onto cookie sheets
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until lightly golden
  9. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before moving to wire racks to cool completely
