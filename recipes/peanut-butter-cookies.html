Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Here's a super simple way to make peanut butter cookies. Mix together just 3 ingredients, bake, and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg together in a bowl until smooth
  3. Roll mixture into small balls and arrange on a baking sheet
  4. Flatten each cookie with a fork to make a criss-cross pattern
  5. Bake for 10 minute
  6. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes
