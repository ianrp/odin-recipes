Sugar Cookies

Description

Satisfy a sweet tooth in a flash with these sugar cookies. Feel free to add chocolate candy on top.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda, baking powder in a bowl
  3. Cream butter and sugar until smooth in a large bowl
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  5. Blend in dry ingredients
  6. Roll teapoonfuls of dough into balls, place onto cookie sheets
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until golden
  8. Let stand for 2 minutes before removing from cookie sheet
